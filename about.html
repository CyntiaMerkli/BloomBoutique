About Us

At BloomBoutique, we believe in the beauty of flowers to brighten every moment. Founded in 2023 by floral enthusiasts, our mission is to deliver fresh, exquisite bouquets anywhere you need them.

Our Story

BloomBoutique is a school project website for a fake flower shop. The site is meant to show various flowers, extra items, and give customers the option to build their own bouquet. On this site you can register an account, login and logout of your account, add products to your cart, checkout, and view your orders. Users can also leave messages for the admin and choose among different options for their products.

The website also includes an admin side. You must hard code a user as an admin member on the bloomboutique database. When an admin account is logged in, they gain access to the admin page, where they can change the website theme manage products, view customer messages, and view customer orders. Only admin can access these pages, no user account has access.

All pictures used throughout the website were either taken by myself or taken from copyright free picture websites.
